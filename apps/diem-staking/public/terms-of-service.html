1. About These Terms

These DIEM Provider Capacity Program Terms ("DIEM Terms") govern access to and use of the DIEM provider-capacity program available through diem.antseed.com or any related interface, smart contract, documentation, or program page that references these DIEM Terms (the "Program"). The Program is separate from the general AntSeed Protocol Terms of Service. The AntSeed Protocol is open-source, peer-to-peer infrastructure. The Program is a specific capacity-participation program connected to provider infrastructure that may operate on or through the AntSeed Protocol. By accessing the Program, connecting a wallet, locking, staking, depositing, delegating, or otherwise using DIEM in connection with the Program, claiming USDC allocations, claiming token incentives, or interacting with related smart contracts, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to these DIEM Terms. 2. Nature of the Program

The Program allows eligible participants to lock or stake DIEM so that such DIEM may be used as provider capacity, collateral, participation weight, or other capacity-support mechanism for one or more provider nodes or provider infrastructure connected to the AntSeed Protocol. If the relevant provider infrastructure processes paid requests and receives USDC through protocol settlement, eligible participants may receive USDC allocations according to the applicable smart-contract logic and Program parameters, after operator fees, costs, deductions, reserves, or other applicable rules. USDC allocations are not guaranteed. They depend on actual paid usage of the relevant provider infrastructure, network demand, buyer behavior, pricing, competition, uptime, operator performance, upstream services, infrastructure costs, smart-contract functionality, legal restrictions, and other factors. USDC allocations may be zero. The Program is experimental. It may be modified, paused, limited, migrated, replaced, or discontinued for technical, legal, regulatory, security, economic, commercial, operational, governance, or other reasons.
